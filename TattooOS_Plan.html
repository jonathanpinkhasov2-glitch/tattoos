01 — What it is

TattooOS in one sentence

Online booking with deposit collection, digital consent forms, and client management — built for tattoo artists, not copied from a hair salon app.

✕ Without TattooOS
  • DM back-and-forth for 3 days just to set a date
  • Manually request deposits via Venmo, hope they pay
  • Paper consent forms that get lost or damaged
  • Client no-shows, you lose the slot and the money
  • No idea how much revenue you made this month
  • Starting completely fresh with each new client
✓ With TattooOS
  • One link — client books, deposit auto-collected
  • Stripe payout direct to your bank every Friday
  • Digital consent form sent & signed from any phone
  • No-shows protected — deposit held upfront
  • Revenue dashboard with exact numbers instantly
  • Full client history, allergies, notes in one place

Why tattoo specifically

$1.6B/year US industry. 80%+ of artists run their businesses through Instagram DMs + paper. Every existing tool (Vagaro, Booksy, Square) was built for salons and retrofitted. TattooOS is the first product that speaks the language: placement, size, style, cover-ups, health flags for consent forms, healed photo management. That specificity is the moat.

03 — Acquisition

How to get the first 100 customers

Phase 1: First 10 paying customers (Weeks 1–4)

Goal: $390 MRR = proof of concept. One channel: Instagram cold DMs.

DM script that converts — copy this exactly "Hey [name] — I built a booking tool specifically for tattoo artists. Handles the whole flow: client books online, pays deposit automatically, signs consent form digitally. Free for 2 weeks, takes 5 min to set up. Want to try it? tattooos-sand.vercel.app"

Target: Artists with 2K–50K followers who post "DM to book" or "booking open." Search #tattooartist + city (NYC, LA, Miami, Chicago, Austin, Atlanta). Send 50 DMs/day. Expected response: 5–10%. One 15-min onboarding call per respondent = paid customer.

Phase 2: Scale to 100 customers (Month 2–3)

📸
Instagram Content
@tattoo_os — daily posts. Before/after workflow comparisons, Reels of the booking form, artist testimonials. Hashtags: #tattooartist #tattoobusiness #tattoobooking. 1 post/day, 3 Reels/week.
🎵
TikTok
Tattoo content crushes on TikTok. "I built software for tattoo artists" (founder story), screen recordings with voiceover, "POV: your booking page looks like this" demos. Post raw and authentic — 3–5×/week.
🔴
Reddit
Post in r/tattooartists, r/TattooApprentice. Don't spam — write genuinely useful content ("How I reduced no-shows by 80%") and mention TattooOS naturally. One good post = 200+ signups.
🏪
Tattoo Supply Partnerships
Email 10 suppliers (Kingpin, Painful Pleasures, Electrum, Cheyenne). Ask to be in their artist newsletters. These companies email thousands of artists who trust their recommendations.
🏆
Tattoo Conventions
Attend the next convention in a driveable city (Hell City, Baltimore Tattoo, Empire State, Villain Arts). Artists concentrated in one place. Hand out cards with the free trial URL. Highest-density pipeline available.

Phase 3: $10K MRR (Month 4–6)

Referral Program

Paying artist refers someone → they pay 2 months → referrer gets 1 month free. Tattoo artists trust peers more than any ad. Becomes highest-converting channel at scale.

Studio Outreach

1 Studio plan ($79) = 4 artists. Cold email studio Instagram accounts. Higher ACV per deal. Studios influence their artists — one deal can bring 4 users at once.
